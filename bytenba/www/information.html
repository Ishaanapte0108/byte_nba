Are you a dedicated professor looking to streamline your involvement in courses and effortlessly manage appraisal forms? Appraispro is your solution!

What is Appraispro?
Appraispro is a specialized web platform designed exclusively for professors, aimed at simplifying the process of tracking your engagement in courses and ensuring the timely completion of important appraisal forms. We understand the complexities of academic life and have created a user-friendly, efficient, and secure system to make your job easier.

Key Features:
Effortless Tracking: With Appraispro, you can easily monitor your involvement in various courses, ensuring you stay on top of your academic responsibilities. Timely Form Completion: No more missed deadlines! Appraispro sends timely reminders and helps you complete appraisal forms accurately and promptly. Data Security: We prioritize the confidentiality and security of your data. Your information is stored safely and accessible only to authorized users. User-Friendly Interface: Our platform is designed with simplicity in mind. You don't need to be tech-savvy to use Appraispro effectively.

Why Choose Appraispro?
Appraispro was developed by a team of computer engineering students who understand the unique needs of professors. Our platform is powered by the versatile Frappe framework, providing robustness, flexibility, and scalability. Join us on this journey towards a more efficient and stress-free academic experience.
